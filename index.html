Privacy Policy — Off The Ball

Last updated: 24 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how the Off The Ball mobile app ("the app") handles your information. The app is developed by Fraser Geddes ("we", "us"). The short version: the app collects nothing about you and sends nothing anywhere.

What information the app collects

The app stores the following information on your device only, using your phone's local storage:

  • Your name, position, kit number, and chosen current/goal level (entered during onboarding)
  • A profile picture if you choose to set one
  • A history of saved matches and fartlek sessions (distance, top speed, sprint count, duration, and — when GPS is granted — your path on the in-app pitch)
  • App settings (sound and vibration toggles)

This information never leaves your device. We do not have access to it, and it is not transmitted to any server.

Location data

With your permission, the app uses your device's GPS during matches and fartlek runs to calculate distance, current speed, top speed, and the path drawn on the in-app pitch. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. If you deny location permission, the app still runs but distance and speed will be recorded as zero.

Photo library

If you tap the avatar to set a profile picture, the app reads the single image you select. No other photos are accessed and no images are uploaded anywhere — the selected image is referenced locally on your device.

Accounts and registration

The app has no sign-up, login, or user account. You can use every feature without creating an account.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking services. It does not contain any advertising SDKs and does not collect anonymous or pseudonymous usage data.

Third-party services

The app uses Expo's hosted update service to check for new versions of the app's code when launched. This check sends only technical information needed to deliver an update (the app's platform identifier and version) and does not include any personal data or content you create in the app.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Deleting your data

Because everything the app stores lives on your device, you can remove all of it by uninstalling the app. iOS will delete the app's local storage at that point. There is no other copy of your data anywhere.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date. Since the app has no way to contact you, please check this page if you want to confirm the latest version.
